The tool should have two agents: 

The Visualizer Agent:
	•	The Visualizer performs EDA for the palmerpenguins dataset (https://allisonhorst.github.io/palmerpenguins/).
	•	The goal of the Visualizer is to investigate the dataset, uncover structure, detect anomalies, and test assumptions. It helps determine appropriate modeling techniques and reveals patterns before formal analysis.
	•	The Visualizer should provide two kinds of outcomes: 
	◦	Statistical summaries

The Critic Agent: 

	•	The Critic Agent judges the outcomes of the Visualizer according to the following rubric:


| Dimension     | Needs Work                              | Solid                                 | Excellent                                        |
|---------------|-----------------------------------------|---------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
|Clarity        |Hard to read/interpret                   |Readable with minor effort             |Immediately clear                                 |
|Insight Depth  |Surface stats only.                      |At least one non-obvious finding       |Multiple meaningful insights                      |
|Completeness   |Major gaps in coverage                   |Distributions and relationships covered|Shape, outliers, and missingness are all addressed|
|Aesthetics     |Cluttered or default styling.            |Clean with proper labels               |Polished and consistent                           |


The tool should have a button to run the Visualizer and display its results, and another button to run the Critic and display its results. 

The tool should run on a local browser.
